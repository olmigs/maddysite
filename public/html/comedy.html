comedy

Maddy is the editor-in-chief of the comedy zine, Kicked Out!, featuring work by performers from Kickstand Comedy Space.

Maddy performs in weekly & monthly shows at Kickstand Comedy. She does stand-up, improv and character shows.

She was on Kickstand’s student house team “Soup’s Up!” from Summer 2019 ‘til Spring 2020. “Soup’s Up!” has continued as an indie team.

Maddy also makes comedy videos on Instagram.
