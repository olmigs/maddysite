writing

Maddy writes personal essays, prose, and plays. She also writes sketch & stand-up comedy.

Read her zines here.

Her play “The Deep End” won for its region at the Kennedy Center College Theatre Festival in 2017. It was also a finalist for the Gary Garrison Ten-Minute Play Award at the same festival. “The Deep End” was later produced at Fir Acres Theatre in 2018.
