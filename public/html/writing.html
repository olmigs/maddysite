writing

My play “The Deep End” won for its region at the Kennedy Center American College Theatre Festival in 2017 and has been produced at Fir Acres Theatre.

I’ve been writing fiction since childhood, but I discovered my love for dramatic writing in college. Most of my work has a comedic edge. I’m published several times in the humor section of my college newspaper, “The Pioneer Log.” I do improv comedy as well, which I consider “writing on your feet.” I was on an improv team called “Scripted” at Lewis & Clark College for three years. Now I take classes and perform at Kickstand Comedy Space in Portland. I’ve also been known to do stand-up comedy when I’m feeling brave (or had a beer).
